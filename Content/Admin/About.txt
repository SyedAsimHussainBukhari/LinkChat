
            <h2>Link to Chat....</h2>
            This website provides a social networking, in which users create links, chat with their beloved ones, view their profiles, edit own profiles, manage account etc.
            The distinguishing feature of this site are RandChat and Criterian Searching.
            In <strong> RandChat </strong>, users would be able to chat with randomly selected people from their surroundings,
            this would create a way to connect with more and more peoples of your sorrounding.
            In <strong>Criterian Chat</strong>,  user would be able to search people of their taste, by some criteria of their interest.
